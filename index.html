Campus Life

The School

Was established in 1986.

Constable School is an Elementary School in Kendall Park, NJ, USA. It was at one point, a blue ribbon school. Constable teaches kids from grades K-5, and teaches them about CARES. Cooperation, Assertion, Responsibility, Empathy, and Self control. The school also helps awarenesses for many different things, raises money for shelters, has many clubs, and even has a wide variety of field trips!

School Achievements

Concerts
Constable School has a concert twice a year!

Constable has a concert in February and one in June. Members of Choir, Chamber Choir, Wind Symphony, (Advanced) Orchestra, and Concert Band all perform in these concerts. These concerts also happen twice, individually. One in the day, for the students and teachers to see, and one in the night, for family. There are other clubs as well, and one that performs elsewhere is Jazz Band.

Clubs
Constable School has many different clubs.

If you read the previous tab, you'll know a bit more about this. Constable School has many different clubs. Some of them perform in concerts, such as members of Choir, Chamber Choir, Wind Symphony, (Advanced) Orchestra, Concert Band, and Jazz Band. Of course, there are other clubs. Student Congress helps plan Spirit Days, Safety Patrol helps people be safe, Yearbook Members help edit the yearbook, Morning Announcers help do the announcements, and there is a group for Plays! You can't be all of these, but as I know, you can be part of quite a lot of these clubs! And, they are back and ready for more, after the pandemic of COVID-19.

Clubs
Constable School has participated in an Invention Convention!

Constable School has put out many flyers for various reasons. Bathroom hygene, field trips, art galleries, scoliosis checkups, but they also participate in the Invention Convention. Everyone entering either creates an innovation or an invention. There is a slideshow to show the differences, what to do, and what is expectable. Tons of kids enter, and all of them have amazing creations. It helps exercise STEAM, as well!
