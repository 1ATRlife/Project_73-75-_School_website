Campus Life

The School

Was established in 1986.

Constable School is an Elementary School in Kendall Park, NJ, USA. It was at one point, a blue ribbon school. Constable teaches kids from grades K-5, and teaches them about CARES. Cooperation, Assertion, Responsibility, Empathy, and Self control. The school also helps awarenesses for many different things, raises money for shelters, has many clubs, and even has a wide variety of field trips!
